Privacy Policy

How Damini Estate collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Damini Real Estate L.L.C ("Damini Estate", "we", "us") operates the website daminiestate.ae. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data transparently and in line with applicable UAE data protection law.

What we collect

  • Information you give us: your name, email, phone number, and the contents of any enquiry or newsletter sign-up you submit.
  • Technical information: basic, non-identifying data such as approximate location (country/city), device and browser type, and referring page, used to operate and secure the site.
  • Marketing attribution: if you arrive via a campaign, we may record the campaign parameters (e.g. UTM tags) attached to your visit.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide our real estate services.
  • To send you information you requested, such as our newsletter (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To operate, secure, and improve our website.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations as a RERA-licensed brokerage.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate — for example our CRM (GoHighLevel), email delivery, and hosting (Cloudflare) — and only to the extent needed to provide the service. These providers are bound to protect your data. We may also disclose information where required by law.

Cookies & tracking

Our website uses only the minimal storage needed to function (for example, remembering your language preference). We honour browser "Do Not Track" and Global Privacy Control signals where applicable.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymised.
