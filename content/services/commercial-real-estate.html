Commercial property in Dubai, done properly

Commercial real estate rewards detail. Whether you are an owner-occupier buying your own office or an investor chasing yield, the difference between a good and a poor outcome usually comes down to location, tenant quality and lease structure, not just the headline price. We look at the things that actually drive a commercial return: footfall and accessibility for retail, floor efficiency and parking for offices, and the strength and length of any in-place lease for an income asset.

Commercial yields in Dubai can sit above residential, but they come with longer void risk and more complex leasing, so going in with clear numbers matters. We help you weigh an owner-occupier purchase (control your premises, build equity instead of paying rent) against a leased investment asset (income from day one, but tied to a tenant), and we structure the search around your sector, headcount and budget rather than whatever happens to be listed.
