How to use these calculators

These four tools answer the questions we are asked most. The mortgage calculator estimates your monthly payment, total interest and total cost from the price, down payment, rate and term. The rental-yield calculator shows gross and net yield once you account for vacancy and running costs, the gap that catches most first-time investors. The ROI calculator combines cash-on-cash return with capital growth for a fuller picture, and the service-charge estimator gives a rough annual figure by size and property type.

They are deliberately simple and indicative. Real numbers depend on the specific building, the lender, the developer payment plan and current DLD fees, which is exactly what we model with you on a real property. Use these to sketch the shape of a deal, then let us pressure-test it.
