Residency that comes with the right purchase

The UAE Golden Visa gives a long-term, renewable residency, and a qualifying property purchase is one of the most straightforward routes to it. The headline threshold is an AED 2 million property, which covers a wide range of homes across Dubai, and the visa can extend to your spouse and children. It is one of the reasons so many investors buy here: the asset and the residency come together.

Our role is to make the property side of that route clean. We check that a specific property genuinely qualifies, point you to homes at or above the threshold that are also good buys in their own right (you want a sound investment, not just a visa ticket), and coordinate with the right people on the application paperwork. Beyond the visa, we help with the practical side of landing in Dubai: the areas that fit your life, schools, and getting set up, so the move itself is smooth, not just the residency stamp.

Visa rules and thresholds are set by the UAE government and can change. We confirm current eligibility for your situation and work alongside licensed processing partners on the formal application.

Golden Visa questions
